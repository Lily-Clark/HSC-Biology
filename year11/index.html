Year 11 — Preliminary Biology

The Preliminary course lays the groundwork for everything in Year 12: cell structure and function, how organisms are organised, biological diversity, and ecosystem dynamics.

Modules

Module 1

Cells as the Basis of Life

Cell structure, the fluid mosaic model, diffusion, osmosis, active transport and enzymes.

 Module 2

Organisation of Living Things

Organisation of cells, nutrient and gas requirements, photosynthesis, digestion, and transport systems.

 Module 3

Biological Diversity

Effects of environment on organisms, adaptations, and the Theory of Evolution by Natural Selection.

 Module 4

Ecosystem Dynamics

Abiotic and biotic factors, sampling techniques, past ecosystem change, and human impacts.

Working Scientifically

Working Scientifically skills (questioning, investigating, evaluating, communicating) are assessed across every module, not as a stand-alone topic. Look for the Working Scientifically callouts inside each module's practice questions.
