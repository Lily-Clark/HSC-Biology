Year 12 — HSC Biology

The HSC course builds on Year 11, moving into inheritance, genetic technologies, and the biology of infectious and non-infectious disease.

Modules

Module 5

Heredity

Reproduction, mitosis and meiosis, DNA replication, and patterns of inheritance.

 Module 6

Genetic Change

Mutation, biotechnology, genetic technologies and their effects on populations and society.

 Module 7

Infectious Disease

Pathogens, transmission, immunity, and the prevention and control of infectious disease.

 Module 8

Non-infectious Disease and Disorders

Homeostasis, causes of non-infectious disease, and epidemiology.

Depth study

Every module includes at least one depth study opportunity. Use the practice question sections as a starting point for extension work, and see each module's Resources section for depth-study-friendly sources.
